What's in Each News Category

  • Oil Markets — daily price action, inventory reports (EIA, API), and supply/demand commentary across crude oil benchmarks.
  • OPEC — OPEC+ ministerial meetings, quota decisions, compliance updates, and statements from Saudi Arabia, the UAE, Russia and other key producers.
  • Natural Gas — Henry Hub and global LNG pricing, EIA storage reports, and weather-driven demand stories.
  • Refining — refinery utilization, planned and unplanned outages, crack spreads, and downstream margin pressure.
  • Extraction — upstream production news from U.S. shale, Brazilian pre-salt, Guyana, Permian Basin and global producers.
  • International — geopolitical risk, sanctions, tanker disruptions, and global trade flow shifts.
  • Technology — energy transition, EVs, renewables, hydrogen, carbon capture and other long-term demand stories.

Energy News FAQ

Where do these articles come from?

Articles are aggregated from major financial wire services (Reuters, Bloomberg, AP), specialist energy publications, the U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA), OPEC press releases and a curated list of global energy outlets. Each card shows the source attribution and links back to the original article.

How often is the news feed refreshed?

The feed is refreshed continuously throughout the trading day. New stories typically appear within a few minutes of publication.

Can I get news for a specific commodity?

For commodity-specific news, visit each benchmark's dedicated page — for example, WTI Crude Oil, Brent Crude Oil or Natural Gas. Each page filters the news feed to that commodity.
